Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: an onion and two cloves of garlic
  • Tomato Sauce: store bought sauce will work just fine
  • Sugar: two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices and seasoning: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: store bought is fine
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg: 1 egg
